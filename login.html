User Guide

1. Login Screen Overview

The login screen includes the following elements:

  • • Email Input: Enter your registered email.
  • • Password Input: Enter your password.
  • • Login Button: Tap to log in.
  • • Forgot Password: Use this option to reset your password.
  • • Activate Account: Tap this button if you don’t have an account yet.
2. Activating Your Account

If you don’t have an account, follow these steps:

  • 1. Tap Activate Account on the Login screen.
  • 2. Provide the following information:
    • - Email Address: Enter your valid email.
    • - Activation Code: Enter the code provided to you.
  • 3. Once activation is successful, you’ll gain access to a 7-day trial.
  • 4. Return to the Login screen to log in.
3. Logging In

Once your account is activated:

  • 1. Enter your email in the Email field.
  • 2. Enter your password in the Password field.
  • 3. Tap the Login button.
4. Forgot Password?

If you forgot your password, use the Forgot Password option:

  • 1. Tap the Forgot Password button.
  • 2. Follow the instructions to reset your password.
  • 3. Use your new password to log in.
This guide ensures a smooth start for all users while exploring the app's features.
